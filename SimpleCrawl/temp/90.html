Description

SUMMARY:

Responsible for process development of plasma based etch and deposition processes for a variety of materials and applications while maintaining high customer satisfaction and safety. Assigned projects will be both at customer sites as well as Plasma-Therm LLC. Travel (up to 80%) will be required.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Design, perform and analyze process modifications aimed at meeting customer requirements and project goals, at customer sites
  • Prepare reports detailing results of field process activities
  • Conduct gap analysis between standard Plasma-Therm equipment performance and Customer specified requirements
  • Travel (up to 80%) to Customer sites to achieve Final Acceptance Testing (FAT) requirements and / or project goals. This may include extended periods travel on longer term projects
  • Provide technical support and trouble shooting on Etch and Deposition products in the field to customers, field service engineers, and the process engineering group
  • Perform process set-up and demonstration runs on Customer samples to illustrate standard capabilities of Plasma-Therm Etch and Deposition equipment
  • Assist in new product development activities for Etch and Deposition as necessary
  • Assist in technical sales support for process performance
  • Other duties as assigned
  • It is every employee’s responsibility to be aware of the applicable Safety & Health, Quality and Environmental Process of the Company

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:

None

Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Chemistry, Physics, or related discipline is required and
  • Two years post educational experience in semiconductor processing is required
  • Must possess a valid driver’s license, clean driving record, major credit card (for business travel), and passport

SKILLS AND ABILITIES:

  • Ability to independently design, develop and test processes based on requirements set forth by Plasma-Therm or Plasma-Therm’s customers
  • Ability to communicate effectively interdepartmentally throughout the Company, and externally with customers, vendors, and the general public regarding equipment, processes, and applications is required
  • Ability to travel extensively (up to 80%) and for extended periods (1 month or more) as required to support Customer related process projects is required
  • Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions
  • Ability to independently drive process changes while at customer sites
  • Must have working knowledge of current required computer skills used in a networked office environment

WORK ENVIRONMENT AND PHYSICAL DEMANDS:

  • The normal work environment is typical of a business office, manufacturing laboratory, and support areas such as production, test, and stockroom.
  • The incumbent is routinely required to sit, stand, walk, touch, see, and hear.
  • The incumbent may occasionally lift or move up to 25 pounds.
  • While performing the regular duties of the position, the incumbent may be exposed to moving mechanical parts, risk of electrical shock, and possible exposure to toxic or caustic chemicals. The incumbent must be able to wear safety equipment and follow safety instructions.
