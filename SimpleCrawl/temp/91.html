Process Engineer

Apply Requisition ID 103889BR Date updated 10/05/2017
Work as part of a multifunctional team in the area of wafer processing of compound semiconductor materials using photolithography, chemical etching, deposition and advanced metrology technologies. The candidate will be responsible for process health (yield, throughput), review and maintain statistical process control (SPC), generate work instructions and train operators as well as new process development.

Required Skills:
  • 4-6 years of professional engineering experience.
  • Sound knowledge of basic engineering principles, theories, and concepts
  • Experience with semiconductor processing equipment, methods, and processes
  • Experience with metrology equipment
  • Experience with a cleanroom work environment (Class 100)
  • Good mechanical aptitude and/or chemical handling ability
  • Excellent computer literacy (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Effective communication and technical writing skills
  • Knowledge of Statistical Process Control (SPC)and Design of Experiments (DOE/ANOVA)


Desired Skills:
  • Experience with II-VI and III-V materials processing
  • Matlab proficiency.
  • Familiarity and experience with device design and modeling tools.
  • Ability to read and interpret mechanical drawings or schematics
  • Visual Basic, G code, C+, or other computer programming experience
  • Understanding of basic semiconductor physics

Required Education: BS in Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical) or Materials Science

Desired Education: MS or PhD in Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical) or Materials Science

This position requires a U.S. person or the ability to obtain an Export Authorization from the appropriate government agency for non-U.S. persons. 103889
Business Unit Profile

Raytheon Space and Airborne Systems (SAS) builds radars and other sensors for aircraft, spacecraft and ships. The business also provides communications and electronic warfare solutions and performs research in areas ranging from linguistics to quantum computing. SAS is headquartered in McKinney, Texas USA. As a global business, our leaders must have the ability to understand, embrace and operate in a multicultural world -- in the marketplace and the workplace. We strive to hire people who reflect our communities and embrace diversity and inclusion to advance our culture, develop our employees, and grow our business.

Raytheon is headquartered in Waltham, Massachusetts. Follow us on Twitter.
