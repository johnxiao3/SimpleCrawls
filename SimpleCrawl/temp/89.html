CVD Process Engineer

Micron
Date: Oct 9, 2017
Location: Manassas, VA, US
Description:

Req Id: 95423 

As a CVD Process Engineer at Micron, you will maintain CVD processes by supporting manufacturing and increasing equipment up time. In this position, you will respond to production issues, implement solutions, reduce costs, and improve yields.  You will be expected to perform Process Development work on new parts.

Additionally, you will use statistical process controls and data analysis to support and monitor the performance of tools. Your other responsibilities will include identifying and resolving defect and tool issues and assisting area technicians with routine maintenance. In addition to improving engineering reaction mechanisms, you will communicate procedural changes to engineers and production personnel.

 

Successful candidates for this position will have:

  • Good data summary and analysis skills.
  • Good communication, documentation, and organizational skills.
  • An innovative and self-motivated personality.
  • A basic understanding of device theory.
  • Strong process knowledge; preferred candidates will have CVD process experience on 300mm AMAT, LAM and TEL tools.
  • Previous semiconductor fabrication engineering experience or applied knowledge in a manufacturing environment.
  • Understanding of Statistical Process Control and exposure to statistics.
  • A strong mechanical aptitude.
  • Good time-management skills.
  • A high level of comfort working in a fast-paced team environment.

 

Education:

BS in Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or equivalent semiconductor industry experience is required; however, other engineering disciplines may be considered.
