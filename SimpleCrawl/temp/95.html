Process Engineer

Date: Oct 2, 2017

Location: Canton, NY, US, 13617

Req #: 6881

Corning is one of the world’s leading innovators in materials science. For more than 160 years, Corning has applied its unparalleled expertise in specialty glass, ceramics, and optical physics to develop products that have created new industries and transformed people’s lives.

Corning succeeds through sustained investment in R&D, a unique combination of material and process innovation, and close collaboration with customers to solve tough technology challenges.

The Specialty Materials segment manufactures products that provide more than 150 material formulations for glass, glass ceramics and fluoride crystals to meet demand for unique customer needs. Consequently, this segment operates in a wide variety of commercial and industrial markets that include display optics and components, semiconductor optics components, aerospace and defense, astronomy, ophthalmic products, telecommunications components and cover glass that is optimized for portable display devices.

 

Summary:

This is a unique opportunity for a ceramic, glass or materials engineer in the area of advanced optical materials and products. The position requires a self-motivated individual with process engineering experience to oversee and assist in the development and manufacture of low volume, high precision glass optics. The successful candidate must have a professional work ethic, strong hands-on design and process engineering skills, and a desire to work in a small team environment.

 

Scope of Position:

  • Provide process engineering support for mirror manufacturing operations for the purpose of improving both existing and developing processes
  • Provide mirror department process leadership focused on process understanding, process improvement, new technology and/or new product lines
  • Provide process documentation and operating instructions
  • Develop best practices, routines and innovative solutions to improve processes and quality of output and reduce risk
  • Assess processes, take measurements and interpret data
  • Develop and support standard operating procedures, documentation, and audits for manufacturing operations processes that will comply with ISO-based practices
  • Develop Experimental Plans/Reports as required to support process improvement and product development
  • Troubleshoot quality issues to determine root cause and implement solutions
  • Engineering lead supporting quotes of mirror programs to customers
  • Maintaining a clean, organized, and safe work environment

 

Travel:  <10%

 

Experience:

  • 2 to 5 years process engineering experience

 

Skills / Qualifications:

  • BS degree in Engineering.   Degree in Ceramic, Glass or Material Engineering highly preferred)
  • Excellent technical skills
  • Knowledge of process related standards
  • Analytical thinker with interpersonal skills
  • Strong computer skills; Knowledge of SolidWorks and CAD skills
  • A teamwork mindset - ability to work/interact with a variety of personnel and backgrounds
  • Good judgment - ability to prioritize properly, work independently and efficiently, and to multitask in a fast-paced team environment
  • Thoroughness in executing assigned tasks; attention to detail and willingness to learn
  • Excellent observation and data recording skills with understanding of equipment, testing, and experimental procedures

 

We prohibit discrimination on the basis of  race, color, gender, age, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, disability, or veteran status or any other legally protected status.


Nearest Major Market: Massena
