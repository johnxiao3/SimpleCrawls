Job Description

ROLE PROFILE

 

Job Title: Research & Development Scientist

 

Major Focus:   Research, design and develop new products, new capabilities, and improve existing products with powder and fiber metals to meet current and future market needs.  

 

Key Responsibilities:

Major Challenges:
  1. Develop new products and capabilities in line with the plans and growth strategy of the company and customer needs.
  2. Successfully manage projects from ideation to launch using Stage-Gate
  3. Develop techno-economic analyses for investments and demonstrate returns through successful project execution
  4. Author technical literature as appropriate to develop reputation and market presence.  

 
  1. Develop novel green-forming capabilities based on new polymer-based formulations
  2. Leverage existing products and forming technologies within and outside of Mott to develop new products/capabilities
  3. Understand key stakeholders and achieving cross-functional alignment on project plans/timelines

Expected Performance Outcomes:

Required Experience:
  1. New forming capabilities developed to make porous metal sintered products within 12 months
  2. Timeline and revenue goals for new product introductions achieved
  3. High-performance project teams developed for 2 new project launches within 12 months
  1. MS or PhD in Metallurgy, Polymers, Ceramics, Chemistry or Materials Science & Engineering
  2. 2-5 years’ experience in Manufacturing/Engineering organization
  3. Extensive hands-on experience in metal injection molding -  synthesis and processing of ceramics/metals/polymers
  4. Experience with net shape forming of metals/ceramics/polymers utilizing Injection Molding, Extrusion (thermoplastic or water-based) and other green-forming methods.
  5. In-depth knowledgeable of colloidal chemistry, dispersion of slurries, de-binding and sintering processes.

 

Technical Knowledge/Skills:

Selection Criteria:
  1. Project Management
  2. Knowledge of colloidal chemistry and advanced materials processing
  3. Metal injection molding
  4. Computer literacy with databases, word processing
  5. Proven ability to write and present technical papers.
  6. Ability to write and file patents as appropriate

 

 
  1. Strategic mindset
  2. Goal driven
  3. Business Acumen
  4. Results oriented
  5. Problem Solver
  6.  Persistent / Tenacious
  7. Self-motivated
  8. Team Player
  9. Organized
  10. Attention to detail

 

COMPANY

Mott is a high precision filtration company trusted by the world’s best technical and performance brands across many industries including healthcare, food/beverage, semiconductors, and aerospace/defense. The company’s strategic vision is to deliver technological breakthroughs where none currently exist.  

 

Established in 1959 and located in Farmington, Connecticut (90 miles from NYC and Boston), the company is 100% employee owned and is poised for significant expansion over the next 5 years. Three years ago, the company undertook an extensive strategic planning process which has resulted in a realignment of the organization and has driven significant growth.

 

This is a terrific opportunity for a R&D Scientist that wants to drive positive change and work in a dynamic, bureaucracy free environment.
