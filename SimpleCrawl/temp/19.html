Entry Level Process Engineer

Tracking Code
301-879
Job Description

WaferTech is an integrated circuit (IC) semiconductor foundry in the United States. Our customers (other businesses) partner with WaferTech to manufacture the computer chips that the client has designed. The chips that we make end up in a wide variety of products that the average consumer relies upon in their daily lives. Cell phones, pacemakers, cars, and security are some examples of how our semiconductors are used.

Located in Camas, Washington, 30 minutes northeast of Portland, Oregon, we are backed by the technological prowess of our parent company, Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC), creator and world leader in the semiconductor integrated circuit foundry industry.


The Process Engineer will be a sustaining/development engineer in a fast paced, semiconductor manufacturing area.  Responsibilities will include improving and enhancing processes, which include constant problem solving, data analysis and system set up.  There are two process engineering positions open.

 

Main duties include:

  • Sustain production operations
  • Continue yield and quality improvement
  • New process development
  • Cost reduction programs
Required Skills
  • Ability to understand production equipment, metrology, electrical measurement and inspection of tools
  • Understanding of process flow
  • Strong verbal and written communication
Required Experience

Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering or Material Science

Job Location
Camas, Washington, United States
Position Type
Full-Time/Regular
