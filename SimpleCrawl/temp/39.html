PVD/IMP Manufacturing Process Engineer

US-UT-Lehi
4 days ago
Job ID
2017-1551
Category
Engineering

Overview

Manufacturing Process Engineers are responsible for partnering with the ROC, Support, PEE, Manufacturing Equipment Engineers and other area’s engineering teams to perform, lead, and manage area process optimization projects.  Manufacturing Process Engineers are also responsible for benchmarking with other network sites and enabling best known method (BKM) solutions.

  • Completing all required safety training and conducting all work in accordance with IM Flash safety policies
  • Partnering with stakeholders to identify opportunities for area optimization in reference to process items
  • Proactively identifying, analyzing, and improving upon existing task, workflow, or operations within the area related to process
  • Designing and coordinating optimization projects with other areas
  • Partnering with management to develop applications to improve manufacturing metrics
  • Presenting findings and recommending solutions to area performance gaps
  • Serving as data and report consultants with customers and stakeholders
  • Driving optimization by researching and using new data modeling techniques or tools
  • Driving projects to ensure that deliverables are completed within the required timeframe
  • Identifying BKMs and lessons learned and sharing them both locally and globally
  • Assuming additional duties and projects as directed by the supervisor
  • Optimizing and implementing best-in-class predictive models and decision tools to address business needs 
  • Communicating data findings to diverse audiences
  • Ensuring successful implementation of projects into business processes
  • Seeking out novel methodologies and tools to better predict and track manufacturing key performance indicators
  • Driving the collection of new data and the refinement of existing data sources
  • Evaluating existing processes and configuring manufacturing systems to reduce cost, improve sustainability and develop best practices within the production process.
  • Developing best practices, reports, dashboards, and other data visualizations
  • Training shift teams on BKMs and rolling out new business processes.
  • Helping improve area quality through metrics, analytics, and business process improvements.

Qualifications

  • Proven ability to communicate complex analysis in a succinct and actionable manner
  • Strong organizational skills and demonstrated ability to complete projects effectively
  • Strong understanding of statistics, data analysis, and problem solving
  • Proven skills in working effectively with team members from different disciplines
  • Intermediate understanding of SPC principles and SPACE navigation
  • Intermediate data extraction, analysis, and reporting skills including SQL
  • Proven ability in multi-tasking complex projects
  • Demonstrated critical thinking skills
  • Demonstrated analytical problem solving skills
  • Demonstrated written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated ability to be detailed oriented and follow complex procedures
  • Proven ability to prioritize responsibilities and manage multiple tasks
  • Intermediate PC skills
  • Solid understanding of production line management techniques and theories
  • Demonstrated ability to learn fundamental semiconductor production floor concepts and operations
  • Demonstrated ability to continuously learn and self-improve
  • Demonstrated ability to identify, develop, propose, and implement effective plans to improve overall efficiency
  • Demonstrate the ability to organize and efficiently facilitate meetings and project teams
  • Strong knowledge of and ability to apply Manufacturing Science (scheduling, inventory management, capacity planning, etc.)
  • Demonstrated understanding and improvement of production metrics and line performance

 

Education and Experience: BS in Industrial Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Engineering Related Field (ChemE, MechE, MSE, etc.) or equivalent experience.  Knowledge of the semiconductor manufacturing process in regards to automation & manufacturing efficiency is preferred.

 

 

All Team members must meet minimum criminal background standards as established by IMFT and the Department of Homeland Security.
